Who else is logged in?

In addition to finding information about your own account, Unix includes utilities for finding out about the other users. One of these is the who utility, which returns a list of the other users that are currently logged in. By default, this list will be printed to the terminal.

Syntax

who [options]

Examples

who
who -q
who --count

Exercise

Try entering the two examples above into the console. Note how the output generated by the second two examples differes from the first. -q and --count are two different ways of entering the same option, which causes the output to include the total number of logged in users.
